The NYC Sound Project is mapping the soundscape of NYC. We need your help to tag sounds from our urban sound database.

We are training our artificial ears to identify sounds within the urban soundscapes of NYC, so the more we train the better our ear will!

Help us by telling us what you hear within the soundscape and where you hear it.

Listen to the recording and select the region where you hear a particular sound, then tell us what that sound is.
